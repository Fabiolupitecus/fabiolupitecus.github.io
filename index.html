Hi There! I'm Fabian

I'm a Motion & Graphic Experience Designer from Colombia and I currently work at The Imagination House in Orlando, FL.

As a Motion Experience Designer I not only create graphics and 3D animations. I also explore and take advantage of the latest software and technology available, to conceptualize and execute immersive storytelling experiences. I have created several experiences for fortune 500 companies in the United States and across the globe.

Take a look my portfolio and feel free to contact me with any questions.
